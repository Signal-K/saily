## Arc 2–4 completion screen copy (all 4 characters)

Copy follows the same structure as arc 1: headline / outcome (2–3 lines) / unlock hook.

---

### ZIX

**Arc 2 — Settling In**
> Headline: "Verdant Paradise: Fully Explored"
> Outcome: "Zix has finished cataloguing every beach, cove, and suspiciously named coastal formation on the planet. She has filed a formal correction with the travel agency. They have not responded."
> Hook: "Arc 3 unlocks: Zix gets a message. Someone needs her help finding *their* Verdant Paradise."

**Arc 3 — The Guide**
> Headline: "Expert Traveller, Unofficial Guide"
> Outcome: "Three other lost tourists have now found their destinations thanks to Zix's navigational notes. She is still waiting for the travel agency to respond."
> Hook: "Arc 4 unlocks: The travel agency has finally replied. It is not what Zix expected."

**Arc 4 — Full Circle**
> Headline: "Greetings from Everywhere"
> Outcome: "Zix has been back to the wrong star system twice — intentionally, this time. She says the sulphur moon has grown on her. The brochure, she has decided, was not entirely wrong."
> Hook: "Zix's full journey is now complete. Thank you for getting her there."

---

### BRIX

**Arc 2 — Second Run**
> Headline: "Supply Run Two: Filed and Approved"
> Outcome: "Seventeen new depot candidates identified across two sectors. The Corps has promoted Brix to Survey Lead. She is treating this as a logistics problem to be solved efficiently."
> Hook: "Arc 3 unlocks: The Corps has a harder assignment. Three depots are missing."

**Arc 3 — Recovery Mission**
> Headline: "Three Depots Located"
> Outcome: "The missing depots were not missing — they were misfiled. Brix has corrected the records and submitted a proposal for a standardised filing system. It has been noted."
> Hook: "Arc 4 unlocks: The final survey. Brix's last run before rotation."

**Arc 4 — Final Run**
> Headline: "Supply Corps Survey: Complete"
> Outcome: "Brix's final survey log has been filed. Forty-two depot sites, six sectors, zero unsolved queries. She has already requested her next assignment."
> Hook: "Commander Brix's full log is complete. The Corps thanks you."

### PIP

**Arc 2 — Building a Practice**
> Headline: "Third Client. Third Distinction."
> Outcome: "Pip's survey reports are being shared between clients without her asking. She has started charging for rush jobs. Her instructor says this is progress."
> Hook: "Arc 3 unlocks: A difficult client. Pip's first complaint."

**Arc 3 — The Hard One**
> Headline: "Complaint Resolved, Reputation Intact"
> Outcome: "The client's complaint was about a planet that turned out to be exactly as Pip described — they had misread the report. Pip wrote a guide for clients on how to read survey reports. Her instructor filed it as required reading."
> Hook: "Arc 4 unlocks: Pip's instructor has a retirement gift for her."

**Arc 4 — The Handoff**
> Headline: "Fully Qualified. Fully Independent."
> Outcome: "Pip's instructor has retired. Her last act was to refer her own remaining clients to Pip. The exam board has asked Pip to help write the new certification criteria."
> Hook: "Pip's full arc is complete. She started nervous. She ended qualified."

---

### CARTA

**Arc 2 — New Sector**
> Headline: "Sector Two: Charted"
> Outcome: "The second sector is mapped. It had more errors in the existing charts than the first. The Cartographer has begun keeping a running tally. It is currently at eleven."
> Hook: "Arc 3 unlocks: A major discrepancy. The Cartographer's own earlier chart is wrong."

**Arc 3 — Self-Correction**
> Headline: "Chart Corrected. Error Logged."
> Outcome: "The Cartographer found an error in her own early work — a planet's orbital parameters recorded incorrectly. She corrected it, logged the error, and updated the standard. This is, she notes, exactly what the correction process is for."
> Hook: "Arc 4 unlocks: The final unmapped region. The chart is almost complete."

**Arc 4 — The Complete Chart**
> Headline: "Sector Survey: Complete"
> Outcome: "The last blank space has been replaced with data. The chart now covers the full survey region — verified, corrected, and filed. 'Probably fine' appears nowhere in it."
> Hook: "The Cartographer's full chart is complete. Every blank filled. Every correction logged."
